Pimples
Causes: Fecal impaction (coprostatis), stress, excessive intake of oily / heavy food, excessive testosterone secretion, constitutionally excessive heat, digestive problems, ischemia (stagnated blood).

Treatment: Decomposition of body fat by improving circulation, suppression of testosterone secretion, applying antipyretic to lower physical constitutional fever, fixing fecal impaction (coprostatis).
