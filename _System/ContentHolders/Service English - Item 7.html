Urology (Prostate Enlargement, Cystitis)

(Prostate Enlargement, Cystitis)

Prostatitis
Causes: Bacterial infections, prostate fluid excretion disorders, excessive drinking, aging, overworking.
Symptoms: Feeling of residual urine, dysuria, testicular discomfort.
Treatments: Bacterial treatments (herbal medicine and acupuncture)

Prostate Enlargement (Benign)
Causes: Aging, prostatitis.
Symptoms: Feeling of residual urine, difficulty urinating, frequent urination.
Treatments: Improve blood flow through extension of urethra (herbal medicine and acupuncture).

Cystitis
Causes: Straining, bacterial infections, poor fundamentals.
Symptoms: Frequent urination, dysuria, hematuria.
Treatments: Bacterial treatment and basic physical reinforcement (herbal medications and acupuncture), steady rest.

Incontinence
Causes: Aging, post-natal.
Symptoms: Unable to hold / control urination. Urination when coughing or laughing.
Treatments: Bladder / sphincter reinforcement.

Bed Wetting
Causes: Congenital bladder / sphincter weakness, stress, mental and behavioral disorders.
Symptoms: Even after age 5, unable to control urination while sleeping through the night.
Treatments: Mental stability, reinforce the bladder / sphincter.
